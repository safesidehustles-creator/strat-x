📉 What "Setup Quality" Is NOT
The 0–100 score rates setup cleanliness, not win probability. A 95-score setup can still lose. The Strat gives you defined triggers and invalidation — that's the edge. Discipline in cutting losers is what compounds an account, not any single trade.
